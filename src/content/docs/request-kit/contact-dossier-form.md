This page provides the minimal contact information and disclosure text required when submitting a prototype request to SpQE.

SpQE stands for **Specification-to-Prototype Qualification Engine** and is pronounced **Speky**.

## Purpose of This Page

This page is not a prototype specification form.

The prototype itself must be described in the **SpQE Prototype Request Form**:

[SpQE Prototype Request Form](/spqe-lab/request-kit/prototype-request-form/)


## What to Send

When submitting a prototype request, send:

1. the completed SpecBlock JSON;
2. the minimal contact information below;
3. the full disclosure and confirmation text below.

Incomplete contact information or missing disclosures may result in no follow-up or additional processing delay.

## Minimal Contact Information

Copy the following block, complete it manually, and include it with your SpecBlock submission.

```text
Contact information

Name:
Organization or legal entity:
Role:
Email(if different):
Preferred contact channel: email...
```

## Free Prototype Offer Disclosures

Copy and include the full text below with your submission.

```text
Free prototype offer disclosures

I understand that this request concerns a free prototype offer.

I understand that the free prototype offer is generally limited to one prototype per company, organization, or legal entity, unless SpQE decides otherwise.

I understand that the offer is intended for a non-commercial showcase context only.

I understand that the submission may be declined by SpQE for feasibility, scope, timing, or capacity reasons.
```

## Submitter Confirmations

Copy and include the full text below with your submission.

```text
Submitter confirmations

I confirm that the information provided with this submission is accurate to the best of my knowledge.

I confirm that no confidential data has been included unless I am authorized to share it.

I confirm that no sensitive personal data has been included unless I am authorized to share it.

I confirm that I am authorized to submit this request on behalf of the company, organization, or legal entity named in the contact information.

I understand that review by SpQE does not create a commercial commitment, service agreement, delivery obligation, or contractual relationship.
```

## Submission Email

Send the completed SpecBlock JSON and the contact/disclosure text to:

```text
contact@spqe.yahoo.fr
```

## Submission Checklist

Before sending, check that:

* the SpecBlock JSON is attached or pasted clearly;
* the contact information is complete;
* the free prototype offer disclosures are included in full;
* the submitter confirmations are included in full;
* no confidential or sensitive information is included unless strictly necessary and authorized.

Submissions that do not include the required contact information and disclosures may be left unanswered or may require additional review time.
